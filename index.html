Schedule Details Event Schedules

09:00 - 09:15 Opening

Introduction

Listen to an introduction to the third instalment of the AWS Community Day - Sri Lanka, and explore what's to come throughout the session.

09:15 - 10:00 Keynote

Derek Bingham

Listen to an amazing keynote from a Senior Developer Advocate, directly from AWS!

10:00 - 10:45 Session

Alex DeBrie

Understanding DynamoDB for Fun and Profit

Gain an insight on using DynamoDB properly by understanding its foundational concepts, underlying architecture and effectively utilize these fundamentals to made complex data relationships in mission-critical applications.

10:45 - 11:30 Session

Lakindu Hewawasam

Building Scalable & Decoupled Backends with AWS Serverless

A common issue of building serverless applications is assuming that the service will automatically meet your workload. However, even this has its limits. Hence, it is essential to understand the limits of scalability, and architect serverless applications to ensure they serve mission-critical workloads.

11:30 - 12:30 Break

Lunch Break

Take a step back, digest the first half of this amazing day. Grab a snack and come tune in for the second half of this day!

12:30 - 13:15 Session

Lahiru Hewawasam

Getting Started With Zero Trust On AWS

Gone are the days of a perimeter-based approach to security. Therefore, it's essential to look into why a traditional security model is ineffective and understand how to leverage existing AWS services to begin your zero trust journey.

13:15 - 14:00 Session

Geethika Guruge

Serverless Architectures...The Good, The Bad and The Ugly

Serverless promises to make cloud development faster, cheaper, and more reliable; however, a badly designed serverless application can mean exactly the opposite of this. Learn about the good, the bad and the ugly of serverless use cases and patterns to ensure your serverless applications are built to last.

14:00 - 14:45 Session

Arshad Zackariya

Secure Your Network using AWS Network Firewall

In the world of Cloud Networking, Security takes the major pillar of it. Tune in to understand the AWS Network Firewall with a hands-on demonstration using some real world use cases.

14:45 - 15:30 Session

Yan Cui

How To Choose The Right Messaging Service For Your Workload

At the heart of every event-driven architecture is a conduit for messages to flow through. AWS offers many services that can act as conduits - EventBridge, SNS, SQS, Kinesis, DynamoDB streams, MSK, IoT Core, and Amazon MQ, to name a few!
